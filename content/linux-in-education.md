Linux and open-source software have fundamentally changed access to education. By removing cost barriers and providing powerful, customizable tools, Linux enables students and educators worldwide to learn, teach, and innovate without proprietary software licensing constraints.

This section covers Linux in academic settings, beginner-friendly learning paths, and how open-source tools are transforming computer science education globally.

- **Beginner Resources**: Learning Linux from the ground up
- **Practical Skills**: Hands-on tutorials for essential Linux competencies
- **Global Impact**: How open-source reduces barriers to quality education
- **Raspberry Pi & Maker Culture**: Affordable hardware enabling STEM learning
